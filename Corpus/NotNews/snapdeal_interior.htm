iBall Slide WQ149R 2-in-1 with Tablet+Hard Keyboard (Intel Atom Z3735F- 2GB RAM- 32GBeMMC- 10.1 Inches Touch- Windows 8.1) (Black)

Work, Play and entertainment, the iball Slide WQ149R has it all to be your new companion. This trendy 2-in-1 tablet with keyboard is the rightmost choice for users who are always-on-the-go. Sporting a sleek body along with dynamic features, it is sure to take your computing experience to a completely new level. Get the power of Tablet and Laptop together with iball Slide WQ149R.

                          

Power of Windows 8.1

Loaded with Windows 8.1 Operating System, the iBall Slide WQ149R delivers brilliant performance in every task. Also, it gives you the flexibility to treat your device the way you want and that too just from the beginning. Change the background colour, create multiple user accounts and group the tiles as per your requirement, this 2-in-1 tablet-cum-laptop enables you to do this and a lot more. Besides, the iball Slide comes with Intel Atom Processor Z3735F clocked at a speed up to 1.83 GHz to deliver amazing performance in each task. Further, the 2GB RAM assures of seamless performance.

 

Huge Storage Capacity

 

Thanks to the 32GB built-in storage, the iball Slide WQ149R tablet allows users to store a huge amount of data without any fuss. It is provided with a Micro SD slot with up to 64GB expandable memory. Right from your entertainment stuff to your work data including flies, presentations and documents, stay assured to save all your data with ease.

                              

 

Optional 3G

 

This 2-in-1 Tablet + Keyboard provide users with the provision to use 3G any time on Ultra-Stick. This enables users to upgrade it to 3G any time.

                                    

 

Aesthetic Features

 

Use it as a Tablet or a Laptop as per your needs and requirements. The iball Slide WQ149R features a hard detachable keyboard with touchpad and normal USB port to convert it into a productive Laptop. This Full Capacitive multi-touch 2-in-1 slide tablet comes with 10.1 inches IPS Screen with a display resolution of 1280 x 80 pixels to deliver an outstanding viewing experience.

                            

Camera

 

Make your chat sessions more interesting with the built-in 2MP Full HD Front Camera and 5 MP Rear Camera. Also, the camera features Autofocus capability as well as supports 720p/1080p at 60 FPS.

                               

Connect with Ease

Provided with 802.11b/g/n wireless connection along with in-built Bluetooth technology, the iball Slide can be wirelessly connected to other Bluetooth-enabled devices like smartphones or printers. Also, users can connect various devices via Micro USB port, Direct USB, Micro HDMI and the USB OTG functionality.

                                   

Safety comes first with iball Slide WQ149R

This 2-in-1 tablet is provided with a default Windows Defender Anti-virus, which protects the system from spywares, viruses and other unwanted software. Additionally, it is also equipped with Family Security, Firewall and Privacy features.

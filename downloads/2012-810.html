Lindsay Lohan may have wrapped her Elizabeth Taylor biopic, but she's still certainly channeling America's princess of poise.

Lohan was spotted at her hotel pool in Hollywood, wearing a sheer-paneled white swimsuit and stretching out on a chaise lounge.

We're happy to see Lohan relaxing these days, especially after she was found non-responsive in June in a California hotel room. It turned out that Lohan was exhausted from filming her forthcoming flick, "Liz & Dick," for several days straight.

The 26-year-old, who just finished filming the 2013 indie thriller "The Canyons," is rumored to be in pre-production for "Scary Movie 5." She better catch up on some (staged) R&R while she can.

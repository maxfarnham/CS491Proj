Penelope Cruz Stuns In Red Leather Jumpsuit On The Set Of 'Zoolander 2'

Earth to "Zoolander" fans, Penélope Cruz is red hot right now.

The Spanish actress made her debut on the set of the much anticipated sequel over the weekend in a skintight red leather jumpsuit:

Deadline.com first announced that Cruz would star in "Zoolander 2" in November, and co-star Ben Stiller confirmed the news earlier this month with a #TBT Instagram photo of “Little Penny.”

Stiller and Owen Wilson will return to their iconic roles as male supermodels Derek Zoolander and Hansel McDonald, respectively. The two actors announced the sequel in March by walking the runway (in character) at the Valentino Fall/Winter 2015 show at Paris Fashion Week.

Cruz and her co-stars filmed a few scenes in Rome on Saturday night, in which the actress can be seen handling a red sports bike:

“Zoolander 2” is expected to arrive in theaters on Feb. 12, 2016.

'Suits': 'End Game' Has Harvey And Mike On A Mission To Bring Stephen Down (VIDEO)

The Huffington Post  |  By Maggie Furlong
Posted: 09/03/2013 1:35 pm EDT Updated: 09/03/2013 1:35 pm EDT
  • Share
  • Tweet
  • Email
  • Comment
  • tumblr

"Suits" has seriously kicked it up a notch, and this week's episode "End Game" (Tues., Sept. 3 at 10 p.m. ET on USA) finds Harvey (Gabriel Macht) and Mike (Patrick J. Adams) going head-to-head with their new nemesis: Stephen.

We've got an exclusive sneak peek (above) of the guys hatching their plan. Should they tell their client Ava, who's on trial for murder, that one of their slimy associates actually committed the crime? Harvey decides that they need more proof before doing that, but with only two days left until trial, there's no way Stephen will come clean. And there's no way he wants to deal with Harvey, who just fought him in the men's room.

But Harvey, of course, knows "someone else" who can tell them the whole story. Who do you think he's going to? And how do you think Stephen will be punished? Will this be the final nail in the merger coffin?

"Suits" airs Tuesdays at 10 p.m. ET on USA.
